The purpose of this project is to analyze the companies in the NASDAQ-100, an index which actually includes 103 companies, and is composed of the largest non-financial companies listed on the NASDAQ. The data for the tickers comes from Quandl and includes relevant stock information between 1/1/17 and 1/1/18. Select a ticker from the dropdown below and click "Analyze."
