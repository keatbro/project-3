Taking a look a the NASDAQ-100.

The purpose of this project is to analyze the companies in the NASDAQ-100, an index which actually includes 103 companies, and is composed of the largest non-financial companies listed on the NASDAQ. The data for the tickers comes from Quandl and includes relevant stock information between January 1, 2017 and January 1, 2018.

Select a ticker from the dropdown below and click "Analyze" to get started.

If you're interested in some more in-depth analysis on a ticker, check out the "Analysis" page to examine the effectiveness of a simple breakout strategy.
